Welcome to Adoring Jensen Ackles - A fansite dedicated to the amazing Texan actor & musician Jensen Ross Ackles Jensen is best known for his portayal of Dean Winchester in the WB/CW dark fantasy Drama TV series 'Supernatural' which ran from 2005-2020 & Soldier Boy/Ben in the Amazon Prime TV series 'The Boys'Jensen also forms one half of the band 'Radio Company' with Steve Carlson & owns the 'Family Business Beer Company' with his wife Danneel Ackles & brother-in-law, Gino Graul. Jensen's current & upcoming TV shows include Tracker, Vought Rising, Countdown & Season five of The Boys. Keep checking back as we bring you the latest media & news on Jensen & his projects.
